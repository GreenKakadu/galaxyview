Battle Simulator

The Battle Simulator calculates the odds that a particular battle will be won or lost. There are several ways to assign ships to attacker or defender groups. (Note that there may be more than one nation involved in a battle, and in fact the Battle Simulator will allow you to put ships from a single nation on opposite sides of a battle, but the Battle Simulator assumes that all attacker groups are at war with all defender groups.) After ships are assigned to attacker and defender groups, the battle is simulated a set number of times (default 100 simulations) and the results are displayed in totals and percentages of battles won, lost or drawn.

Attacker and defender groups are displayed in tables identical to those used in the Groups Table.
